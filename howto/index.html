how to make fettachine alfredo

ingredients

list of needed items

  • 2-3 cups your favorite pasta or preferably fettachine
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cup parmesan
  • optional: pepper for better taste

steps

  1. Cook pasta to your liking.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter over medium heat in a double boiler of or small to medium sauce pan.
  3. Add minced garlic and cream.
  4. Reduce heat to medium/low and allow to cook for roughly 5 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle in cheese and pepper.(if you are using pepper)
  6. Continue cooking until cheese has belnded and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Pour over pasta and enjoy.
